The service, which builds upon our recently launched intruder intervention*, extends the technology to outside the home. Now, when your system is armed during plan hours and motion is detected outside, agents can act to deter crimes in progress. They can see and speak directly to potential intruders in real time using the live feed and 2-way audio, and trigger camera sirens — all while capturing video evidence of what's happening.
